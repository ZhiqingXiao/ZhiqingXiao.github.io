Zhiqing Ranked 2nd in an in-house AI Competition

2019/04/13

Zhiqing participated in an in-house AI competition (JPMC ROAR CIB SLC Prize) and ranked the 2nd among ~170 contestants from 20+ cities.

Contestants were required to respond to real-time requests for predicting bond volume at future time points. The judge platform calculated the total errors of predictions for each bot within designated 24 successive hours. The bot with smaller error ranked higher.

The competition was hosted in JPMorgan ROAR platform.
