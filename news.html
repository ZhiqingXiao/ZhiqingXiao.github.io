Zhiqing Published a First-author Paper in IEEE Transactions on Information Theory

2015

Paper "Distributed Multilevel Diversity Coding", authored by Zhiqing Xiao, Jun Chen, Yunzhou Li, and Jing Wang, is published online in IEEE Transactions on Information Theory on Sep. 9, 2015. Prof. Jun Chen is the supervisor and the corresponding author of this paper.

According to wikipedia, "among hundreds of computer science-related journals, IEEE Transactions on Information Theory had the highest ranking and was thus deemed the most prestigious."

Zhiqing Interned at Baidu

2015
